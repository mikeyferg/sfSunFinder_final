About SF Sunfinder

SF SunFinder is for people in San Francisco who want to escape the cold and fog and find some sun!

One of the best things about living in San Francisco is how close you are to so many beautiful sunny places outside of the city. Sometimes you just want to escap eand find some fun. Where should you go? The Easy Bay? Marin? Napa? Palo Alto? Santa Cruz?

SF Sunfinder will help you decide!
